The acronym "PUMA" can stand for Programmable Universal Machine for Assembly. It also refers to a political action committee called People United Means Action. Additionally, in the context of the sporting goods company, "PUMA" is a name chosen by Rudolf Dassler for his company, inspired by the qualities of the puma animal.
